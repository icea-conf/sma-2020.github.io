Aim & Scope

    SMA 2020 will provide an excellent forum for addressing research issues of concern within the Smart Media and Applications. SMA 2020 concentrates on innovative solutions to complex problems in all areas of industry and sciences using advanced techniques in Smart Media and Computer Science Applications. It involves Smart Media, Smart Software Applications, Smart Information, Smart Services, and so on. Authors are invited to submit original papers in all areas related to the Smart Media and Applications.

    Topics will include, but not be limited to, the following areas:

    Smart Information

    • Intelligent computers, cloud computing and distributed and parallel processing systems
    • Artificial intelligence, image processing, computer graphics, audio processing, multimedia and human computer interactions (HCI)
    • Big data, intelligence information processing, information security, mobile information communication and IoT
